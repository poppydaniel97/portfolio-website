PROBLEM

Users donâ€™t have the time or ability to visit physical stores and would rather buy their goods on the go or from home.

SOLUTION

Botanica is an online Garden Centre, helping everyone find products quickly and easily to match their particular needs. The tool allows users to purchase their desired goods and have them delivered to any address. Free return of the goods is possible.

USER STORIES

1. As a returning customer, I want to be able to save items that I cannot buy to a wish list, so that I can purchase them at a later date

2. As a returning customer, I want to see an overview of my previous activity, so that I can view previous purchases and search history

3. As a customer, I want to see what other customers have said about products and how they have rated them, so I can better judge whether the item is right for me

TYPOGRAPHY

Consitent typography is essential in creating any brand. While communicating, two typeface families are chosen; Cinzel Decorative and San Francisco Display.
